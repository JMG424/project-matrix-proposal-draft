The next step is not simply “more agents.”

The next step is a clean implementation sprint: harden the foundation, organize the company source of truth, improve the current agent workflows, and give Tylor and Abel seamless access to Matrix-level context around the priorities that surface in their 1:1s.

90 days to reduce ambiguity before it becomes operational drag.

1. Safer AI Foundation

Credential review, access control, permissions, read-only-first rules, and human approval for sensitive actions.

2. Company Source of Truth

Turn scattered company context, decisions, workflows, and open loops into a usable internal intelligence layer.

3. Leader-Level Utility

Oscar first, then flexible Matrix-enabled work with Tylor and Abel based on what actually matters in their 1:1s.

Proposed Sprint Structure

Read-only first · Approval-gated
Week 1

Security + Access Control

Make the foundation safe before expanding data access or automation.

  • Credential and access audit
  • Permission map
  • Safe integration checklist
Weeks 2–4

Oscar Source of Truth

Turn Oscar’s company context into a usable map for the rest of the system.

  • Company/entity map
  • Decision log format
  • Executive brief rhythm
Weeks 5–10

Tylor + Abel Matrix Access

Use 1:1s to identify priorities, then give each leader relevant Matrix-level context in a clean, professional way.

  • Working-session summaries
  • Role-specific access plan
  • Briefs, maps, specs, and next actions
Weeks 11–13

Consolidation + Roadmap

Bring the workstreams together and turn what was learned into the next Matrix build sequence.

  • Source-of-truth v2
  • Leadership rhythm
  • Next 90-day roadmap

What the sprint includes

  • Security hardening and access planning.
  • Source-of-truth buildout.
  • Focused work with Oscar, then flexible Matrix-enabled sessions with Tylor and Abel.
  • Decision logs, recaps, workflow maps, and implementation priorities.
  • Selected Matrix buildout tied to real business priorities.

Scope boundaries

  • Agents begin read-only: summarize, draft, recommend, and prepare.
  • Sensitive finance, customer-facing, support, compliance, or production actions require human approval.
  • No vague “AI help” bucket without owner, workflow, source system, and business outcome.

Expected Day 90 outcomes

  • Safer AI operating foundation.
  • Company source-of-truth v2.
  • Cleaner leadership operating rhythm.
  • Matrix working views for selected priorities.
  • Practical next-phase Matrix roadmap.
