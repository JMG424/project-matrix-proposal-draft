4Abel — Start with the workflows that hurt

For Abel, the first sprint should stay narrow: pick the workflows that are painful, data-rich, and safe to improve with drafts, alerts, and human approval.

Candidate Abel workflows

  • Packaging procurement trigger → Slack / PO draft: turn reorder triggers into actionable procurement drafts.
  • Inventory reorder intelligence: stockout risk, velocity, lead times, and recommended purchasing actions.
  • Provider 360 profile: NPI, order history, support, relationship notes, and next best action.
  • Customer health score: red/yellow/green churn risk with recommended retention actions.
  • Support intelligence: ticket sentiment, topic, urgency, escalation, and suggested response.
  • Meeting → execution: transcripts converted into decisions, owners, due dates, and project updates.

Safe build pattern for any workflow

  • Spec: APIs, permissions, failure modes, approval rules.
  • Staging: no production credentials, no unsafe write actions.
  • MVP: limited production data with approval gates.
  • Production: automated where safe, human approval where required.
  • Documentation: capture the working pattern so Abel’s team can safely extend what proves useful.

6Working principles

Read-only first

Agents observe, summarize, draft, and recommend before they modify.

Human approval

Outbound, financial, customer-facing, regulatory, and production actions stay reviewed.

No cross-contamination

Finance, compliance, sales, supplier, and operations context stays scoped unless deliberately bridged.
