3Core workstreams

Oscar — command + source of truth

Priorities, decisions, risks, open loops, regulatory movement, competitors, entity map, team visibility, and the executive intelligence rhythm.

Tylor + Able — company intelligence access

Role-based access to Matrix-level company data: shared context, revenue/customer visibility, operating dashboards, and governed answers from the company source of truth.

Able — operations + systems

One high-leverage ops workflow shipped with approval gates: packaging procurement, Ship Day routing, support triage, onboarding surveys, or task extraction.

4Development process

Spec → staging → MVP → production

Define the workflow, test safely, ship a narrow working version, then harden only what proves useful. No vague automation bucket.

Weekly leader 1:1s

Weekly sessions with Oscar, Tylor, and Able surface bottlenecks, review what agents are seeing, and choose the highest-value improvements.

Read-only first

Agents can summarize, draft, recommend, and prepare. Sensitive finance, customer, compliance, or production actions stay human-approved.
